Step 2 — Update your address

9 places you need to update

Check each one off as you go — your progress saves automatically.

🪪 Driver's License & DMV

Update your address at the DMV — required within 30 days in most states. Also update your vehicle registration.

~15 min Go
🗳️ Voter Registration

Stay registered to vote at your new address. Takes 3 minutes online and can be done before your next election.

~3 min Go
🏛️ IRS & Federal Taxes

File IRS Form 8822 to update your address for tax refunds and notices. Also update your state tax agency.

~10 min Go
🏦 Banks & Credit Cards

Log in to each bank and credit card account and update your mailing address so statements reach you.

~20 min Log in to each bank
💊 Social Security

Update your address with the Social Security Administration via your My Social Security account online.

~5 min Go
📦 Amazon & Online Shopping

Update your default shipping address on Amazon, and any other retailers where you have a saved address.

~10 min Amazon
🛡️ Insurance Policies

Contact your auto, health, and life insurance providers. Address changes can affect premiums — especially auto.

~20 min Call or log in to each
👔 Employer / HR

Update your address in your company's HR system so your W-2, pay stubs, and benefits mail reach your new home.

~5 min Update in HR portal
🩺 Doctors & Pharmacy

Call your primary care doctor, dentist, specialists, and pharmacy to update your address and transfer prescriptions if needed.

~15 min Call each provider
